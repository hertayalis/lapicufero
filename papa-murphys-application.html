Papa Murphys Application Online: Jobs &amp; Career Info

One of the most extensive pizza chains, Papa Murphy’s, operates across the United States. It serves as a take and bakes pizza chain. This company often creates in-store pie’s and the patrons cook them at their homes. Papa Murphy’s has more than 1,400 pizza stores in around 37 states across the United States. This pizza chain offers various managerial, entry-level, and first-time job hunters’ capacities.

Check out this article to learn the online job application process for Papa Murphy’s. Candidates will also know about various job roles offered at Papa Murphy’s, its overview, work schedule, benefits, role and responsibilities in this pizza chain.

Operational Hours and Work Schedule at Papa Murphy’s

Papa Murphy’s employs candidates with a minimum of 16 years at the time of submitting their job application form. The shift timing and work schedule at Papa Murphy’s is as follows:

  • Sunday to Thursday: 11:00 a.m. to 12:00 a.m.
  • Friday: 11:00 a.m. to 01:00 a.m.
  • Saturday: 11:00 a.m. to 01:00a.m.

Job Prospects at Papa Murphy’s

The candidates applying for the entry-level posts need to serve the customers with exceptional services and produce exciting menu items per the company’s mandates.

The applicants who don’t have a work history or have worked for a lesser period in the past can also serve the company as crew members. They mainly need to focus on ingredients stock, ensure cleanliness and prepare products as per the customer’s desires.

The candidates applying for upper-level or managerial posts need to hold a qualification degree and work experience. Along with this, they need to be motivated and enthusiastic about the post.

The Job Positions Available at Papa Murphy’s

Papa Murphy’s offers multiple job opportunities to job potentials, including General Restaurant Managers, Assistant Restaurant Managers, and Prep Cooks. It also seeks candidates for Line Cooks, Cashier, and Team Member positions. The readily available job roles at Papa Murphy’s include the following:

Crew Member

  • The candidates applying for the post need to prepare customers’ meals and foods according to the specifications and interact positively with them.
  • The part-time job has flexible hours, and the candidate needs to have flexible schedules for the post considerations.
  • They need to be friendly and also can earn around $8.00 per hour.

Management

  • The position gets further divided into store manager and assistant store manager posts, and the candidate will serve to be on the intermediate position between entry-level and supervisory employees.
  • The candidate needs the store’s marketing and probability decisions, achieving efficiency in labor and inventory costs, maintaining cleanliness, and minimizing wastes.
  • The candidate applying for the post can around $11.00 to $12.00 per hour.

The Online Job Application Process of Papa Murphy’s

Papa Murphy’s allow job seekers to fill up the online job application form as instructed below. Then, candidates can go step by step as guided and submit their form to get the opportunity of getting a job position at Papa Murphy’s.

  • The job applicants can go to Papa Murphy’s career portal and choose the job type to apply for this pizza restaurant chain. Candidates can check out for corporate and in-store positions.
  • It is essential to fill up the required position to apply for a job at Papa Murphy’s pizza restaurant locations.
  • Tap on the “See Store Details” tab to view the details that will appear here. You can check “Store Careers” for an online job application.
  • Complete the online job application process after filling all the blank fields and submit your job application form for Papa Murphy’s.

The candidates can also download the PDF version to apply for a job role at Papa Murphy’s. First, fill up the entire form carefully and review all sections of the form. Then, submit your job application at Papa Murphy’s physical stores after providing your education details, skills, and your signature at the end.

Checking Job Application Status for Papa Murphy’s

The only way to apply for the job is by filling out the application form at the stores. The hiring process of Papa Murphy remains uncomplicated as most of the interviews for the applicants happen on the spot. They need to return the forms as soon as the vacancy lasts for considerations. The hiring decision of the managers is also the conclusion of the interview and gets followed up by the hiring personnel approving the deserving candidates for the post.

Managers give a call back to the deserving candidates by looking at their applications. The candidates who haven’t received any call-backs must contact the hiring personnel to show their interest in the jobs.

Benefits and Perks While Working at Papa Murphy’s

Papa Murphy’s provide its candidates’ multiple employee benefits, including the following:

  • Papa Murphy’s provides team-oriented, fast-paced, and joyful work settings for its employees.
  • Employees at Papa Murphy’s also receive food discounts, uniform provisions, and paid training as job benefits.
  • They also get holidays, paid vacations, flexible scheduling, and competitive wages while working at Papa Murphy’s.
  • Full-time Papa Murphy’s employees also benefit from 401 (K) retirement plans, vision, dental, and medical insurance.

Additional Details of Papa Murphy’s

The chain follows unique yet attractive fundraising policies, which are easy to participate in. They have collaboration with multiple fundraising programs and are donating efficiently for the same. For example, in the North California chains, the owners have to donate specific portions of each purchase to cancer research organizations at the local hospitals.

In its chain in Arizona, anyone who gets stuffed toys gets offered free pizza or $5 off on the purchase. The company gives stuffed animals to children going through traumatic situations. They have also advised their chain owners to find a new direction for the fundraising activities which can be participated for the welfare of society.
