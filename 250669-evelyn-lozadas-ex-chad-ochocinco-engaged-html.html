Evelyn Lozada's Ex Chad Ochocinco Engaged Fans Think His Fiance Looks like the BBWLA Star

 Posted on July 21, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  491 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Evelyn Lozada's ex-husband Chad Ochocinco is now engaged to a lucky woman named Sharelle Rosado. Some fans think the woman in question looks like the former football star's ex-wife.

Chad "Ochocinco" Johnson, the ex of "Basketball Wives LA" star Evelyn Lozada, has finally found a woman he will be spending the rest of his life with. He is now engaged to Sharelle Rosado.

While fans rejoice in Ochocinco's engaged status, some other fans thought the former football star has yet to get over Lozada. Here's what they thought about Rosado.

Chad Ochocinco and Evelyn Lozada at the Maxim Party at Centennial Hall at Fair Park on February 5, 2011 in Dallas, Texas | Photo: Getty Images

The Shade Room earlier shared an Instagram video lifted from Rosado's Instagram Story, where she showed off a large diamond engagement ring from Ochocinco. On top of the video clip, it read, "I said YESSSS."

Many fans were delighted at Rosado and Ochocinco's announcement and sent the newly engaged couple good wishes and congratulatory messages. However, a fan noticed the picture and commented:

"Looks like Evelyn to me."

A user's comment on The Shade Room's Instagram post about Sharelle Rosado. | Photo: instagram.com/theshaderoom

Curious fans took a second look at Rosado's snap and agreed with a fellow fan that the sports & entertainment realtor had an uncanny resemblance to a woman in Ochocinco's past.

One fan noticed the similarities but didn't mentioned any names and just wrote, "you know who." Another one chimed in and said Rosado looked like the "Livin' Lozada" alum.

In an earlier discussion with Brandon Marshall in the "I Am Athlete" YouTube series episode called "Is a Woman There for the Money or is She the One?" Ochocinco spoke about Rosado.

Another Instagram user's comment about Sharelle Rosado looking like Evelyn Lozada. | Photo: instagram.com/theshaderoom

Rosado is also a retired army paratrooper and a veteran of Operation Enduring Freedom or OEF. Ochocinco shared that Rosado is the woman who gave him structure in life.

Ochocinco was linked to many female celebrities, including singer K. Michelle.

ROSADO AS "THE ONE"

When asked if she is the one, Ochocinco explained that if a person calls in the middle of a game and one is willing to press pause to answer the call, then that person is the one. He must have done it many times with Rosado.

As he enters into a new yet familiar chapter in his life, Ochocinco shared that his sweet affection for Rosado doesn't mean to cast a shade on his past relationships. He shared that everybody in his past was "wonderful."

Ochocinco was linked to many female celebrities, including singer K. Michelle. After he proposed to Lozada while playing "Call of Duty," he married the 45-year-old BBWLA star in 2010.

Their marriage ended in 2012 after the former NFL star was charged with domestic violence for reportedly headbutting Lozada during an argument. The former couple shares six children.
