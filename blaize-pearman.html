Introduction :

Blaize Pearman is the younger brother of actress/singer Raven-Symone Christina Pearman. Raven is well-known for her Disney show, “That’s So Raven.” He didn’t have an interest in acting, so fame is more concentrated on his older sister.

Personal Life, Parents and Family Details :

  • Blaize Pearman was born on December 17, 1991, to parents Christopher Pearman and Lydia Gaulden in Ossining, New York. Originally from Atlanta, Georgia, the family had moved there to support Blaize’s older sister, Raven, and her growing acting career.
  • Raven & Blaize’s father, Christopher Pearman, managed his sister Raven all throughout her childhood and teenage years and is usually credited with raising a superstar. He has even authored a book on parenting, “Dream So Big: A Parents Guide to Helping Your Child Believe and Achieve” where he talks about encouraging his children.
  • In the same book, Christopher shared a story about his son Blaize. Christopher used to play the clarinet but gave it up in high school, and his musical genes got passed down to his son, Blaize.
  • At five years old, Blaize was already good at playing the piano, and his dad thought the talent would serve Blaize well in life. He thought he could always have this to fall back to and maybe be a concert pianist or a synthesizer player in a band or he could even just put open up a box, and someone would put money in it. But his parents allowed him to stop when he wanted to quit. Christopher talks about how he shouldn’t have given in so easily.
  • In an interview, his sister Raven was shown a picture of her and Blaize at a younger age, and Raven said that even though she’s the older sister, Blaize is the style guru of the family. She then asked Blaize to keep giving her fashion advice.

Fun Facts, Height and Trivia :

  • According to activity on his Twitter (now called X) page, Blaize seems to be very fond of gaming. He has retweeted many news articles about new gaming consoles and graphic cards.
  • Blaize had a small guest appearance on his sister’s “That’s So Raven” show in the second episode of the first season titled “Party Animal.” He plays a friend of Cory, the on-screen brother of Raven.
  • He also appeared on an episode of Celebrity Family Feud in 2008 along with Raven and Raven’s on-screen parents from the Disney show “That’s So Raven.” The family played to donate to the “American Red Cross.” They played against Wayne Newton and did not win the episode.
  • He played basketball for the AAU league in Atlanta and is a big fan of the Lakers.
  • Blaize along with his family and sister, Raven are often seen together in many posts on Blaize’s Instagram profile.
