Law and Order Organised Crime: Meet the cast of season three

 Posted on July 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  570 words  |  Larita Shotwell

We are loving the new series of Law and Order: Organised Crime, which is currently airing on ABC.

The spin-off series stars Christopher Meloni as SVU detective Elliot Stabler, who returns to New York following his wife's murder. But who else appears in the show? Meet the cast here…

WATCH: Law & Order Special Victim's Unit and Organized Crime trailer

Christopher Meloni as Detective Elliot Stabler

Christopher Meloni stars as Detective Elliot Stabler in the popular spin-off series. The actor took a decade-long break from the franchise, having first starred in Law & Order: Special Victims Unit for 12 years from 1999. 

While the 61-year-old may be best known for his role in the police series, he has starred in numerous other films and shows over the course of his career. 

His big-screen credits include Man of Steel, Wet Hot American Summer and Runaway Bride. Viewers may also recognise him for playing inmate Chris Keller on the HBO prison drama Oz. 

Christopher Meloni stars as Detective Elliot Stabler

Danielle Moné Truitt as Ayanna Bell

Danielle Moné Truitt plays Stabler's partner and squad supervisor Sergeant Ayanna Bell in the series. 

The 41-year-old actress is known for her roles in BET's police series Rebel and Fox's procedural drama Deputy. She has also appeared in episodes of medical drama Chicago Med and crime series Snowfall.

Danielle Moné Truitt plays Sergeant Ayanna Bell

Ainsley Seiger as Jet Slootmaekers

Ainsley Seiger plays NYPD officer and former independent hacker Jet Slootmaekers in the police drama. 

The 24-year-old actress made her screen debut back in 2012 in the short film American Waste. Her role on the police drama is her first television credit. 

Ainsley Seiger plays NYPD officer Jet Slootmaekers

Dylan McDermott as Richard Wheatley

Dylan McDermott plays infamous crime boss Richard Wheatley in the series, who was responsibly for the murder of Angela's son as well as being a suspect in the murder of Stabler's wife. 

The 60-year-old actor is perhaps best known for playing lawyer Bobby Donnell in the legal drama series The Practice, as well as his roles in four seasons of FX horror anthology American Horror Story.

More recently, he played Will Hodges in the award-winning biographical sports drama film, King Richard.

Dylan McDermott plays infamous crime boss Richard Wheatley 

Tamara Taylor as Angela Wheatley

Tamara Taylor plays the troubled Angela Wheatley, who is the ex-wife of Richard Wheatley and former suspect for the murder of Stabler's wife. 

She is known for her role as Dr. Camille Saroyan, head of the Forensic Division, in the forensic crime drama Bones. She also played Sibyl in season seven of Marvel's Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.

Tamara Taylor plays Angela Wheatley

Brent Antonello as Jamie Whelan

Brent Antonello is a new addition to season three and plays Detective Jamie Whelan. 

The 33-year-old is known for playing Hank Sullivan in Dynasty as well as for his portrayal of Jude Kinkade in VH1's Hit the Floor.

Brent Antonello plays Detective Jamie Whelan

Rick Gonzalez as Bobby Reyes

Rick Gonzalez plays Detective Bobby Reyes in the new series. The 43-year-old actor is known for playing Timo Cruz in the biographical sports drama Coach Carter, as well as for portraying Ben Gonzalez in the CW supernatural drama television series Reaper.

Rick Gonzalez plays Detective Bobby Reyes
