Reese Witherspoon, Kerry Washington Gave Joshua Jackson Parenting Advice

 Posted on June 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  330 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Joshua Jackson shared during an interview with Good Morning America that his Little Fires Everywhere costars Reese Witherspoon and Kerry Washington gave excellent parenting advice on set.
  • Jackson is currently expecting his first child with wife Jodie Turner-Smith any day now.
  • "Both of them were incredibly helpful, both in like, finding doctors, OBs, just resources of people to talk to, and then, frankly, just leading by example," said the actor during the interview.

Reese Witherspoon and Kerry Washington made sure that their Little Fires Everywhere costar Joshua Jackson was prepped with proper parenting advice before becoming a father for the first time.

During an interview with Good Morning America, the 41-year-old actor shared some of the advice his A-List co-stars provided him while on set of the hit Hulu series. Jackson is currently expecting his first child with Queen & Slim actress Jodie Turner-Smith any day now.

"Both of them were incredibly helpful, both in like, finding doctors, OBs, just resources of people to talk to, and then, frankly, just leading by example, because the both of them are not just actresses, but they run their own companies, they're both mothers, they've integrated their family life and their professional life in a way that seems very healthy," said Jackson. "So just watching how they navigate all of that, because next time I got to work, I'm gonna be a daddy."

Jackson has also commented on how he and Turner-Smith are managing stress while preparing to welcome their baby amid the ongoing coronavirus pandemic.

"[Jodie is] physically fine and the baby is physically fine so the important things are all good. We're just figuring out how to deal with interacting with doctors and hospitals right now," explained the actor.
