Keke Wyatt Shares New Photo of Her Baby Son Ke'Riah Darring with His Eyes Open

 Posted on June 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  421 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Keke Wyatt is a proud mother as she took to social media to share a photo of her newborn son, K'Riah, who happens to be her tenth child.

Keke Wyatt is a happy mother of 10. The famous R'n'B singer and TV personality recently took to her Instagram page to share an image of her newborn son, Ke'riah.

The image featured Ke'riah looking cozy wrapped in a blanket that had reindeer prints all over it, and it also had a matching cap. The post came equipped with a simple caption, "Finally caught him with his eyes open."

Keke Wyatt flaunting her baby bump at the Black Music Honors | Source: Getty Images

The picture attracted a lot of positive comments from fans of the singer, as several commented on the resemblance between mother and son. Others immediately recognized him as a singer with a fan gushing:

"He is beautiful, AND he looks like he can sing. I want to hear him cry. If he cries on key, then we know he inherited his mother's lungs. He is adorable. He really is."

Ke'riah is the first child the singer and her husband, Zachariah Darring, will be having together since their marriage. The couple had a baby shower to celebrate this in November 2019.

During her union with Ford, Wyatt gave birth to eight kids.

Keke first revealed her pregnancy news in October sharing photos from a maternity shoot which showed the star lying in a bathtub filled with rose petals.

She also posted another snap cradling her burgeoning baby bump in a blue sequin dress. The shower featured a winter-wonderland theme and took place at the Nouveau Bar & Grill in Atlanta.

The event was reportedly coordinated by Erin Jackson and was attended by the couple's family and friends. The proud mother first shared news of his arrival via her Instagram page and the post was a collage.

Occupying the right side was a photo that featured a nude mirror selfie of Wyatt that flaunted her round baby bump while the left side had a picture of her newborn son.

The caption saw Wyatt offering details concerning her baby's birth. According to her, Ke'riah was born on January 6, 2020, and weighed a healthy 7lb and 11 ounces.

Wyatt and Darring got married in 2018 after her divorce from her then-husband, Michael Ford. During their union, Wyatt gave birth to eight kids. Before her marriage to Ford, she was married to Rahmat Morton with whom she had a child as well.
