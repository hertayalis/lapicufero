A New York Times best-selling author and memoirist, Pat Conroy (born October 26, 1945) is best known for writing several critically acclaimed novels and memoirs. Two of his books, The Prince of Tides and The Great Santini, were adapted into films that received Academy Award nominations. He is widely regarded as a seminal figure in the literature of the American South in the late twentieth century.
